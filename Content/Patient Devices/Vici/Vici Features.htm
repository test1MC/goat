Adjusting Speaker Volume and Muting Microphone

The speaker volume may be adjusted and muted directly from the Integrated Speaker Microphone.

  • Adjust the volume by pressing the up and down volume buttons.
  • Mute the microphone by pressing the center button.
    • The lights on the device will flash when microphone is muted.
