Before You Begin

The InTouch TV should be installed by an IT Professional to ensure proper setup and network configuration.

Before setting up your InTouch TV, review the InTouch Telehealth Network Specifications document (MB-14011) (MB-14011) to configure your network to optimize the telehealth consultation experience. If you do not have the Telehealth Network Specifications document, please contact your local InTouch Health Product Specialist.
