Connect to the Wi-Fi

Notes:

  • Before proceeding, consult your IT department for the correct Wi-Fi network name and password.
  • The taskbar is defaulted to Automatically hide and may not be displayed.
  1. Click minimize icon () in the upper right hand corner of the display.

  1. Locate the Wi-Fi icon in the desktop taskbar.
    • - Not connected to Wi-Fi:
      • The is not currently connected to a Wi-Fi network.
      • Select the appropriate Wi-Fi from the list displayed and enter password when prompted.
    • - Connected to Wi-Fi:
      • The is connected to a Wi-FI.
      • Verify the is connected to the correct WI-Fi.
  2. Click the InTouch icon () on the task bar to return to the application screen.
