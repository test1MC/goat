Getting Started

Turning on InTouch Viewpoint

  1. Plug your InTouch Viewpoint device into a power outlet.
  2. Press the Power Button to turn your device on.

CAUTION: The InTouch Viewpoint device should be plugged in whenever it is possible so it is fully charged and ready for a consult. The InTouch Viewpoint will remind you to plug it in by playing the audio message “Please plug me in”.
