Viewpoint iOS Overview

InTouch Viewpoint is InTouch Health Patient Access Device software for use on iPad. Using InTouch iOS Viewpoint app, patients can receive HIPAA compliant audio and visual medical consults from designated remote healthcare professional over the InTouch Telehealth Network. The InTouch iOS Viewpoint sets a new standard for Telehealth work flow management and is designed to deliver fast, convenient, and effective non-acute patient care where and when it is needed. The InTouch iOS Viewpoint enables you to provide a superior experience when patient care matters most.

InTouch Viewpoint Features

  • FDA Class I device and HIPAA compliant.
  • Automatic bandwidth and video quality optimization.
  • Transmits HD video.
  • InTouch Telehealth Network supports standards based H.264 Advanced Video Coding (AVC) connections.
  • iOS 10 support.
  • 24/7 remote support and monitoring

Recommended iPad Models

  • iPad Pro, iPad Air, iPad Mini series*

*Digital Box Zoom feature is only supported on iPad with HD cameras.
