Viewpoint Troubleshooting

Symptom Action
InTouch Viewpoint cannot connect to Wi-Fi. Attempt to connect to Wi-Fi network– See “Changing Wi-Fi Networks (Windows 8 Touch Screen)”. If you are still unable to connect to Wi-Fi, contact your hospital’s IT department.
InTouch Viewpoint screen is black/blank.
  • Verify that the InTouch Viewpoint is plugged in.
  • Quickly press and release power button. If the screen remains black/blank, make sure the device is powered on– See “Turning on InTouch Viewpoint”.
