Viewpoint Getting Started

Turning on InTouch Viewpoint

  1. Plug your InTouch Viewpoint device into a power outlet.
  2. Press the Power Button to turn your device on.

CAUTION: The InTouch Viewpoint device should be plugged in whenever it is possible so it is fully charged and ready for a consult.

  1. Log into your desktop and double-click on the icon named Robot. Once the screen shown below appears, the InTouch Viewpoint software is running.

Windows 8 (Touch Screen)

  1. Swipe your finger inward from the far right side of the device.

  1. Tap the Settings button.
  2. Tap the Wi-Fi button.
  3. Make sure that Wi-Fi is turned on. Tap on the desired Wi-Fi network with your finger.
  4. If required, enter the Wi-Fi network password and click Next. You should now be connected to Wi-Fi. Tap elsewhere on the screen to close Settings.
  5. Tap the information icon on the top right corner of the Viewpoint screen and ensure that the status is Ready before attempting to make a connection.
