Auxiliary AC Power Receptacle (V1 and V2 only)

The Auxiliary AC Power Receptacle is located under the shelf on the top of the Expansion Bay. The receptacle installed is the standard for the country location to which the Lite is delivered.

This receptacle is rated at: InTouch Lite (US) 115VAC, 60Hz, 2.1A; InTouch Lite (International) 230VAC, 50Hz, 1.7A.
