Power Sequence for V3

Power On Sequence

CAUTION: To ensure system readiness, connectivity, and charged batteries, power on the at least two hours before its intended use. This will allow the to check for and install any available software updates.

The should be plugged in and left powered on at all times.

  1. Plug in the power cord:
    • Ensure that the power cord is plugged into an approved wall socket.
  2. Turn On the Power at the Button Panel:
    • Press the power button once.
    • Check to ensure the Display powers up successfully.
    • After a period of self-testing (1-2 minutes), the screen as shown below should appear on the Display.

  1. Turn on the Boom Camera, if required:
    • If the Boom Camera light is amber, the Camera is in standby and will need to be turned on.
    • Press the power button on the Boom Camera Remote Control while pointing at the Boom Camera.
    • The power light should turn green.
  2. Turn on the Boom Camera Display, if required:
    • If the Boom Camera Display has been turned off, you are powering the InTouch Vantage for the first time, or the InTouch Vantage has been in storage, you may need to turn on the display. If the power light is amber, the display is in standby and will need to be turned on.
    • Press the power button on the bottom right edge of the display. The power light should turn green.
    • Confirm that the Display powers up successfully.

Power Off Sequence for V3InTouch Vantage

NOTE: Powering off is not recommended unless instructed to do so by InTouch Technical Support.

Turn Off the Power at the Button Panel:

  1. Press the power button once.
  2. Ensure the Display screen turns off (goes to black, approximately 30-45 seconds).
    • If the is on Screensaver Mode or the Status Info screen, a confirmation dialog box will ask you to confirm shutdown.
  3. To shutdown, press the check button, as instructed on the screen.
    • These instructions must be followed within seven seconds to successfully shutdown the .

    • If you do not press the check button within seven seconds, the will assume shutdown was initiated by accident.

    • You will need to wait one minute to re-initiate shutdown.

    1. Press and hold the main power button for 3-5 seconds to turn off the main power.
      • The power indicator light will turn off.
