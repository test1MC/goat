InTouch Lite Button Panel

1 Power Button Turn the computer on or off. Keep the on at all times unless it is necessary to power off. Power off the device only when instructed by Technical Support. The sounds an alert when the power button is pressed and will ask for confirmation before powering off the device.
2 Stop Video Button Stops the video feed from the InTouch Lite, preventing the from receiving images from the device.
3 Volume Control Used to adjust the speaker volume on the device.
4 CONNECT Button Not functional.
5 DISCONNECT Button Immediately disconnects all remote users. Ends the active session following a disconnect confirmation prompt.
6 Check Button When not in session, pressing the check button displays information on the and network status.
