Environmental Specifications:

Operating:

Designed to operate in an indoor environment suitable for human personnel. (10° to 40° C, 30 to 75% RH, 700 hPa to 1,065 hPa)

Non-operating:

Designed to travel to installations in commercial and cargo airliners and standard ground transportation. (0° to +50° C, 10 to 95% RH, 700 hPa to 1,065 hPa)
