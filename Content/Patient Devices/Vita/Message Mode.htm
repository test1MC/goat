Message Mode

Message Mode allows Vita to display an alert message on its Head and Chest Displays when certain events occur. The Vita Head and Chest Displays as well as the LED indicator lights will turn into a magenta color when in Message Mode. The table below describes some of he messages you might encounter when Vita is in Message Mode and the action to take to resolve the issue.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Message Explanation Action
The Vita is unable to dock because something is in its path.

Clear the area around the dock, then press the Dock button on the Vita chest display to dock the Vita.

NOTE: When the dock is blocked the Vita may AutoDRIVE to a predesignated location such as the nurses’ station.
The Vita is unable to leave the dock because something is in its path. Clear the area around the dock, then press the OK button on the Vita chest display.
Vita has been taken out of its mapped area and is “lost”. Vita must be brought to its docking station by either manually driving it from the Provider Access or by pushing it using the handle on the back of the Vita. NOTE: AutoDRIVE will not be available.
Vita has been powered up off its dock. Vita must be brought to its docking station by either manually driving it using the Provider Access or by pushing it using the handle on the back of the Vita.
The Vita’s map was changed. Manually dock the Vita to the new floor’s dock to continue use.
