Vantage Auxiliary Ports

Audio Ports

The InTouch Vantage includes both audio line input and output ports which allow allows auxiliary audio devices to be connected to the InTouch Vantage. The audio selection and mixing of the audio line input is controlled through the Provider Access Software application.

CAUTIONS:

  • Do not leave equipment connected to any auxiliary connectors. The equipment and/or the InTouch Vantage may be damaged if the InTouch Vantage is moved with equipment connected to an auxiliary port.
  • Do not connect any auxiliary equipment to the InTouch Vantage that would cause or lead to penetration of a sterile field if the InTouch Vantage is moved by a remote user or otherwise.

 

NOTE: The InTouch Vantage V1 audio line input and output ports are stereo jacks; when connecting a monaural audio device you should use a stereo-to-mono adapter to ensure that audio is available to both channels.

Video Ports

The InTouch Vantage expansion bay includes two NTSC Auxiliary Video Input Ports (S-Video and Composite Video connectors) which support the attachment of a variety of devices. This allows the remote physician to view the auxiliary video through the Provider Access Software application.

WARNINGS:

  • The video images transmitted to and displayed on the InTouch Vantage and Provider Access Software may not contain all of the information from the original scene. Video information from the camera is captured, compressed, transmitted, and redisplayed remotely at a different resolution. As a result information from the original scene may be lost.
  • Color reproduction in the transmitted video is not guaranteed. Color reproduction in a video system is a complicated combination of lighting, cameras, and display technology. It should not be assumed that the colors on the display are an exact replication of the actual colors in the scene.
  • Clinical judgment and experience are required to review and interpret images and information transmitted via the InTouch Vantage and Provider Access Software.

CAUTIONs:

  • Adding third party software or hardware to the InTouch Vantage may cause it to malfunction or operate erratically. Excluding those devices designed for connection through existing hardware ports, InTouch Health does not support the addition of third party software or hardware to the InTouch Vantage. Please check with Technical Service prior to installing any other third party software or hardware.
  • Do not leave equipment connected to any auxiliary connectors. The equipment and/or the InTouch Vantage may be damaged if the InTouch Vantage is moved with equipment connected to an auxiliary port.
  • Do not connect any auxiliary equipment to the InTouch Vantage that would cause or lead to penetration of a sterile field if the InTouch Vantage is moved by a remote user or otherwise.
