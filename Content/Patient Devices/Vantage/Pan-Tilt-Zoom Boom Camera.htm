Pan/Tilt/Zoom Boom Camera

The InTouch Vantage Pan/Tilt/Zoom (PTZ) Boom Camera system includes a Camera/Microphone, a Display, and a remote control. The Boom Camera system is powered on whenever the Main power is on. If either the Camera or the Display is turned off (amber standby light is lit), then the component must be turned on. When on (green power light is lit), the Boom Camera will immediately send video to the Boom Display.

Boom Camera and Microphone

The Boom Camera and Microphone are located at the end of the InTouch Vantage Boom. The video selection and microphone selection are both controlled through the Provider Access Software application.

Boom Camera Display

The Boom Camera Display displays video from the Boom Camera. Once the Display Power button is on, the Display will turn on whenever power is applied. There is no audio associated with the Display.

Boom Camera Remote

The Boom Camera Remote Control allows you to control the Boom Camera at times when the InTouch Vantage computer is not powered or when a Provider Access Software login is not available. The Boom Camera Remote Control is located next to the audio input and output ports on the outside of the expansion bay.

CAUTION: Do not use the Boom Camera Remote Control during an active session.

  1. Ensure Boom Camera is on.
  2. Press Camera Select #1.
  3. Use PAN-TILT and ZOOM to achieve the desired view.

Refer to the Sony EVI-D70 Color Video Camera Operating Instructions for important information, operating, and user maintenance procedures.
