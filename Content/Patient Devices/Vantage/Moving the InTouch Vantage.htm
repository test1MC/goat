Moving the InTouch Vantage

Directions:

  1. Prepare to move InTouch Vantage:
    • Ensure that the Boom is completely folded — See “Extending and Folding the InTouch Vantage Boom”.
    • Ensure that Head is set at its lowest position — See “Adjusting the InTouch Vantage Height”.
    • Ensure that the power cord is unplugged and clipped to the power cord shelf hook.
    • Ensure that the wheels are unlocked.
  2. Move to the desired location.
    • Use the handle to move InTouch Vantage.
    • Exercise caution when encountering thresholds.
  3. Lock the wheels.
  4. 4. Plug in the power cord and turn on the InTouch Vantage — See “Power On Sequence”.

WARNINGs:

  • The InTouch Vantage is neither MRI (Magnetic Resonance Imaging) safe nor is it MRI compatible. The InTouch Vantage should only be used in locations where the presence of metal is not controlled.
  • Flammable Anesthetics: The InTouch Vantage is not suitable for use in the presence of any flammable anesthetic mixture with air, or in the presence of a flammable anesthetic mixture with oxygen or nitrous oxide.

Cautions:

  • The InTouch Vantage is a large and heavy piece of equipment. Caution must be exercised to avoid collision with persons, inanimate objects, and critical equipment when moving or repositioning it during use or transport.
  • Observe the overhead Boom when moving or adjusting the position of the InTouch Vantage. Overhead objects such as lights, can be damaged by unexpected collisions.
  • Do not pull down or place any heavy objects on the Boom, especially when the Boom is extended.

  • To ensure system readiness, connectivity, and charged batteries, power on the InTouch Vantage at least two hours before its intended use. This will allow the InTouch Vantage to check for and install any available software updates.

  • Avoid the labeled pinch points on the InTouch Vantage, especially when operating the lifting mechanism, extending or folding the Boom, and during remote sessions when the InTouch Vantage head and the Boom Camera can be moved bmpy a remote user.

  • The InTouch Vantage is not being provided sterile and any potential peripherals attached to the InTouch Vantage may not be provided sterile.
