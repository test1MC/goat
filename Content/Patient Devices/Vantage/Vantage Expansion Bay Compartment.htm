Vantage Expansion Bay Compartment

Under the hinged Laptop Tray is a compartment with the Auxiliary AC Power Receptacle. This receptacle is rated at:

  • InTouch Vantage (North American) – 120VAC, 60Hz, 2.1A
  • InTouch Vantage (International) – 230VAC, 50Hz, 1.7A
  • InTouch Vantage (International) – 230VAC, 60Hz, 1.7A
