Adjusting the Vantage Height

Directions:

  1. Ensure that the InTouch Vantage wheels are locked.
  2. Squeeze the levers and pull up or push down to raise or lower the height.
NOTE: The InTouch Vantage Boom can be placed between 80 inches (2.03 meters) and 111 inches (2.82 meters) off the floor.

CAUTIONS:

  • Avoid the labeled pinch points on the InTouch Vantage, especially when operating the lifting mechanism, extending or folding the Boom, and during remote sessions when the InTouch Vantage head and the Boom Camera can be moved by a remote user.
  • Observe the overhead Boom when moving or adjusting the position of the InTouch Vantage. Overhead objects such as lights, can be damaged by unexpected collisions.
  • Do not pull down or place any heavy objects on the Boom, especially when the Boom is extended.
