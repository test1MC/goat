Vantage Overview

InTouch Vantage V1 Anatomy and Components

Antennae Connects InTouch Vantage to broadband Internet via 802.11 Wi-Fi (a, b, g, or n).
Boom Display Displays video from the Boom Camera.
Directional Microphone Captures Focused Audio mode audio for playback on the Provider Access Software.
Expansion Bay Holds the connectors for approved USB peripheral devices, auxiliary video inputs, audio line input and output, auxiliary AC power receptacle, and hardwire network.
Handle Used for moving or positioning the InTouch Vantage.
Head Camera Captures remote video for viewing at the Provider Access Software.
Head Display Displays the video from the Provider Access Software.
Power Cord Shelf Hook Secures the power cord when moving the InTouch Vantage to a new location.
Power Plug and Cord Used to power the InTouch Vantage and recharge the batteries.
Stereo Microphones Captures Immersive Audio mode audio for playback on the Provider Access Software.
Wheel Locks Locks the front wheels in place when InTouch Vantage is stationary.
