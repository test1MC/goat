Creating a New Patient Encounter

Viewing or Editing a Patient Encounter (Windows)

  1. Scroll through the patient encounter list to find the patient name (by default patients created in the last 24 hours will be shown) or use the Patient Encounter Lookup to search for the patient.
  2. Click on the blue patient name link in the Smart Notes Dashboard to open the patient encounter once the patient name is located,
  3. Click on the various tabs to view or enter information
