Viewing or Editing a Patient Encounter (iOS)

  1. Scroll through the patient encounter list (by default patients created in the last 24 hours will be shown) or use the Filter items... to search for the patient to find the patient name .
  2. Tap anywhere on the encounter in the Smart Notes Dashboard to open the patient encounter once the patient name is located.
    • The search updates each time a character is entered in the Filter items search bar.
  3. Click on the various tabs to view or enter information.
