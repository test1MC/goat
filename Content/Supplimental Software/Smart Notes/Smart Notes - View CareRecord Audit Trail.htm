Smart Notes - View Encounter Audit Trail

Account administrators can verify who and when an encounter was touched and what additions or changes were made to the encounter.

  1. Select the encounter from the Encounter Dashboard.
  2. Click Audit Narrative located at the top right of the encounter screen.

  1. Scroll through the Audit Narrative to view each change made to the patient's encounter.
  2. Click Back to return to the Encounter Dashboard.
