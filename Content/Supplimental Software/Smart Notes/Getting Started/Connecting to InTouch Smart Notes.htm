If you are using an iPad, you can also access InTouch Smart Notes through the InTouch Provider Access Software for iOS.

  1. Login to the InTouch Provider Access Software for iOS app
  2. Tap the My Apps button in the Tab Bar at the bottom of the iPad screen.
  3. Select Smart Notes from the list of available Healthcare Apps.
  4. Enter your Username and Password and click the log in button.
