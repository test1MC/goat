Smart Notes - Signing and Distributing a Consultation Note

Patient data entered into the encounter during an assessment is aggregated and automatically displayed into a medical and billable compliant consultation note. Only data entered by users is displayed on the consultation note. Users can review, then electronically sign the consultation note, then proceed to distribute the note where required.

  1. Click Consult Note on the left navigation menu.
  2. Scroll down to review the displayed consultation note.
  3. Click View unsigned PDF to open a PDF version of the Consultation Note.

  1. Click Sign note if Consultation Note is ready to be signed and distributed or click Continue editing to close the Consult Note PDF to make changes to the encounter.
