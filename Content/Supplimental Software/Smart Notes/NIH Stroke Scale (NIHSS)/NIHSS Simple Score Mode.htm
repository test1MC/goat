NIHSS Simple Score Mode

  1. Click or tap the Simple Score Mode button next to the Total Score field.
    • This will disable all of the Neurological Exam Elements drop-down menus.
  2. Enter the total score.
    • For Windows, enter the Time Encountered by clicking on the calendar icon or the Now button.
    • For iOS, enter the Time Encountered by tapping on the Time Encountered field.
  3. Click or tap Create NIHSS Encounter.
    • The score and the timestamp will be encountered.
    • The NIHSS encountered scores will be shown in a box at the top of the tab.
