iOS Touch Commands

One Finger Tap

  • On the top toolbar: selects a tool.
  • In the viewing area: hides or shows series tray and toolbar.
  • In the series tray: will swap series in display.
  • At the bottom of a navigation zone: will move forward one image; in the top backward one image.

Touch Drag Up/Down

In a navigation zone: will navigate forward and backward through the image stack.

Pinch Zoom

In the viewing area: will zoom in on the displayed image.

Touch Drag Multi-Direction

  • When window level tool is active will modify window level values.
  • When cursor (pan) tool is active and image is zoomed, will pan the image.

Touch Pan

  • When zoom mode is enabled, pressing and dragging will pan the image around the viewport.
  • When window level is enabled, pressing and dragging will modify the window center and width values.
