Overview

's DICOM imaging viewer enables health care professionals to access, manipulate and collaborate in real-time over full quality medical images using any web-browser without installing client software. is a server-based solution that integrates with your existing hospital infrastructure. Once the server is installed, it connects to your PACS or VNA and displays DICOM images within the hospital, securely from remote locations, or as an integrated part of an EHR or portal system.

Intended Use

  • TM is a software application that displays medical image data and associated clinical reports. performs operations relating to the transfer, storage, display, and measurement of image data.
  • allows users to perform image manipulations, including window/level, rotation, zoom, measurement and markup.
  • displays both lossless and lossy compressed images. For lossy images, the medical professional user must determine if the level of loss is acceptable for their purposes.
