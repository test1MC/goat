Unlocking a Patient’s Provider Encounter Form

To manage a patient’s Consult Note that is in use by another Provider, Providers will need to unlock the patient encounter. Follow the steps below to unlock a patient record.

Note: Providers must have permissions established to unlock and edit a patient’s Consult Note; otherwise, the Unlock feature will not be available.

  1. Use the search function on the Encounter List screen to locate the patient.
  2. Click the LOCKED drop-down..
     

Provider does not have permission to unlock

If the Provider does not have permission to unlock Provider Encounter Form, the following dialog box displays:

  1. Contact the Provider who is listed in the dialog and ask them to unlock the patient’s Provider Encounter Form.

  2. Access the Provider Encounter form when the form is again available.

    • The ability to edit a patient’s Provider Encounter Form is dependent on the permissions established for the Provider accessing the Provider Encounter Form.

Provider has permission to unlock

If the Provider has permission to unlock Provider Encounter Form, the following dialog box displays:

  1. Click Unlock when prompted, “If you require urgent access to this form you have the option of unlocking it, but this may result in unsaved changes being discarded.”

 

 

 

 

  1. Click Unlock when prompted, Are you sure you want to unlock Provider Encounter Form?

  2. Access and edit the Provider Encounter Form as needed.
