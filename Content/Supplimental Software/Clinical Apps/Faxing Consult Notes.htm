Faxing Consult Notes

After signing the Consult Note, users are redirected to the Encounter List. If users need to fax the Signed Consult Note, the following steps need to be followed:

  1. Click Signed Consult Note for the note that needs to be faxed.

  1. Click Send Fax to select where to send your Signed Consult Note.

  1. Select the fax contact from the dropdown or enter the fax number where the Signed Consult Note needs to be faxed and click on Send Fax. To exit this form, click on Cancel Fax.

  1. Any faxes sent will be shown in the Signed Consult Note’s log.
