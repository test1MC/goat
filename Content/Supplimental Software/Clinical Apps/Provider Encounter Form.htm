Provider Encounter Form

  • Use the Provider Encounter Form to enter observations, diagnosis and recommendations from the encounter.
  • Enter all relevant patient information according to needs by selecting the appropriate tabs.

Note: Each Tab may require you to scroll down to see all available fields on the Tab.

Patient Tab Descriptions

Patient Tab Descriptions
Patient Tab Tab Description
PT Info Patient Information—contains fields relating to general patient information. Includes: Date of Birth, Medical Record Number and Account Number.
HPI History of Present Illness—Information about patient’s present illness.
PT Hx Patient History—tobacco, drug and alcohol comments; medications and review of systems.
Exam Exam—details about current exam, including Vitals.
Labs Lab Results—details about any completed tests corresponding to the patient.
Imaging Imaging—contains fields to enter information regarding review of patient’s images.
NIHSS National Institutes of Health Stroke Scale—allows users to record the patient’s NIHSS score. The NIHSS recorded scores are shown at the bottom of the tab.
Calcs Calculators—contains various healthcare related tests; including, SEDAN Score, Glasgow Coma Score, ABCD2, CHADS2, NYHA Classification, Hunt and Hess, Fisher Grade, ICH Score, ASPECTS, FOUR Score and Los Angeles Motor Scale.
Alteplase Alteplase t-PA Recommendation—allows user to enter findings related to administering the drug, Alteplase.
Endovascular Endovascular Treatment Eligibility—allows user to determine patient eligibility for Endovascular Treatment.
Recs Recommendations—allows user to enter recommendations based on information about the patient encounter.

Completing the Provider Encounter Form

Complete the Provider Encounter form by clicking Finalize.

NOTES:

  • Once a note has been signed it cannot be edited or deleted. Add an Addendum to a note to identify any changes.
  • Clinical expertise is required to interpret and provide an assessment and recommendation within the system. Independently verify calculator outputs.

Signing and Printing Consult Notes

Patient data entered into InTouch   during a session is auto-populated into a Consult Note. Users can review the patient data captured in the consult, make appropriate changes, then electronically sign and time stamp it to make a permanent record.

  • Make the note a permanent part of the Patient Record by clicking Sign Note.
