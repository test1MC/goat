My Schedules

The My Schedules tab allows the Provider to view their Upcoming and Past Schedules and to edit a selected schedule.

To view a schedule, click the schedule from either the Upcoming Schedules or the Past Schedules tabs.

  • The selected schedule opens.
  • From the selected schedule, select PROVIDED or ASSIGNED
    • PROVIDED - displays schedules that are given by the Provider.
    • Assigned - displays schedules that are assigned by the Receptionist.

 

Adding Availability to a Created Schedule

Provider schedules that have been created by the Receptionist appear in the My Schedules tab of the Provider's screen. These are identified by the Status of Created.

  1. Select the Created schedule.

  1. Click Add Your Availability.

  1. Select the type of schedule from one of the following:
    • Date Range – Enter a start date and time and an end date and time from the available dates on the calender.
    • Multi-Date – Select the available dates and enter a start and end time for each date selected.
    • Recurring Dates – Select an available start date and an available end date with a start time and end time for each date in the selected date range and the frequency of the recurrence.

  1. Click the to add the selected schedule.
    • The entered schedule will appear.
  2. Repeat steps 3 and 4 if necessary.
  3. Click Done Editing when done adding availability.
    • The new dates are displayed.

  1. Click Submit.

  1. Click Yes to submit the schedule and to make it available.

Editing a Schedule

A Provider can edit a schedule that has been assigned to them through the My Schedules tab.

  1. Click Edit ()
  2. Locate the date or dates to remove from the schedule.
  3. Click the x next to the date to remove it from the schedule.
  4. Click Yes, when prompted, to confirm the change to the schedule.
    • The deleted date will become grayed.
