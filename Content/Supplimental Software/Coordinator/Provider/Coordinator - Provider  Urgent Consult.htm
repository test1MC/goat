Coordinator - Provider Urgent Consult

When an Urgent Consult is requested, the provider will be notified that the patient is ready to be seen.

If a mobile phone number has been supplied for text messages, the following text message is received by the provider, "Hello Dr. Donaldson, a patient is ready to be seen for an urgent Telehealth consult at InTouch Clinic #1. Please log on to Provider Access Software to accept or deny this request. Thank you."

The Active Worklists displays all Urgent Consult patients assigned to that provider.

  1. Click Accept to accept the Urgent Consult.
    • Or click Decline to decline the Urgent Consult and to inform the receptionist.
  2. Click Notes to view details about the patient.
  3. Click Connect to begin the consultation.
    • If not already in 's , the will launch and connect to the patient.
  4. Click End Call when done with the consult.
