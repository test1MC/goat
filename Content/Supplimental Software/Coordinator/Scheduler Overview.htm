Roles

Each logon to is assigned one of the following roles:

Role Description
Patient
Scheduler		 The Patient Scheduler role is responsible for establishing a patient's appointment.
Receptionist The Receptionist role is responsible for checking the patient in upon their arrival. This step allows the Medical Assistant to Call In the Patient for their appointment.
Medical Assistant After the patient has been checked in, the Medical Assistant role is responsible for calling the patient into an exam room, taking their vitals and logging the vitals in . Anytime the provider requires the medical assistant to return to an exam room, an alert appears on the medical assistant's screen.
Secondary Provider A Secondary Provider is a role in that is assigned to a member of the medical staff to allow them to remotely access a patient for the purpose of a preliminary consult prior to the primary Provider's consult.
Provider The Provider role is responsible for providing care to the patient. From their Work Flow on , the provider is able to see which patients are ready for examination. The provider is also able to review the patient’s current vitals, taken by the medical assistant, and the patient's vitals' history together with any notes from previous exams logged in the . The provider has the ability to call the medical assistant back into the exam room at any time. Once done with the exam, the provider has the option to schedule a follow-up exam with the patient.
