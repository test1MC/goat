Patient-Side Work-flow App Taking Patient's Vitals

  1. Click on or tap the InTouch device's screen to display the current list of checked-in patients who have been assigned an exam room.

  1. Click or tap Select to enter the patient's vitals.

  1. Enter vitals by clicking or tapping on field and then enter the information in the appropriate fields.
Note: If a device, such as a Welch Allyn, is connected to the device that records vitals, the vitals will automatically be entered in the appropriate fields.
  1. Click or tap Save to save the displayed vitals.
  2. Click or tap Notify Provider to send a message, alerting the Provider that the patient is ready to be seen.

Notes:

  • If the provider has a cell number associated with their account, they will receive an SMS notification when the Notify Provider is tapped.
  • If the Secondary Provider workflow is enabled, only the secondary provider will receive an SMS notification if a cell number is associated with their account.
