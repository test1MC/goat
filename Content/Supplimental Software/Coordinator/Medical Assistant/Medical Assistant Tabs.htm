Medical Assistant Tabs

Medical Assistant Training Video

uses four tabs to help with the flow of patients:

  • Checked in tab
  • Consult Prep tab
  • Ready for Consult tab
  • Complete tab

Checked in tab

The Checked in tab displays the patients who have been checked in for their appointment by the receptionist. These patients are ready to be assigned a room.

Available options

  • Assign Room - select the patient's exam room.

  • Record Vital Signs - opens the vital signs dialog box to enter the patient's vital signs.

  • Notes - opens notes screen to allow entering, editing or viewing of patient's notes.
  • View - view the patient's Details and current Patient Flow.

Consult Prep tab

The Consult Prep tab displays patients that have been assigned to a room OR had their vitals taken. Whichever task is not complete will appear to be completed in that tab.

Available Options

  • Assign Room - select the patient's exam room.
  • Edit Vital Signs or Record Vital Signs - opens the patient's vital signs so they can be viewed or changed.
  • Notes - opens notes screen to allow entering, editing or viewing of patient's notes.
  • View - view the patient's Details and current Patient Flow.

Ready for Consult tab and Complete tab

Ready for Consult tab displays the patients who have been assigned to an exam room AND had their vitals taken. These are patients who are ready to be seen by a provider.

The Completed tab displays the patients who have been seen by a Provider.

Available Options

  • Edit Vital Signs or Record Vital Signs - opens the patient's vital signs so they can be viewed or changed.
  • Notes - opens notes screen to allow entering, editing or viewing of patient's notes.
  • View - view the patient's Details and current Patient Flow.
