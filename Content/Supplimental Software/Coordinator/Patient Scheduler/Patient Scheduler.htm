Scheduling an Appointment - Patient Scheduler

  1. Verify the Urgent Consult Request is not selected.

  • If the bullet is green, click Urgent Consult Request to clear the bullet.
  • See "Coordinator - Scheduling an Urgent Consult" on page 1 to schedule an Urgent Consult.
  1. Select Patient Scheduler from the available tabs.
  2. Click Last Nameor First Name and enter the Patient’s name.
  • The more information entered in the patient search, the more specific the results will be.

  1. Click Search.
  2. Locate the appropriate Patient from the results.
  3. Click on the Patient’s name to select.
  • If unable to locate the Patient in the results, create a new patient:
  1. Click New Patient,
  2. Enter Patient’s information,
  3. Click Create Account when done.
  1. Click New Appointment.
  2. Select the requested Provider from the Providers drop-down and the type of appointment from the Appointment Types drop down then click Select.

  1. Select the appointment day and time, or Immediately, for the next available time.

  1. Select the time for the Appointment from the list of available times.

  1. Verify all information is correct.
  • Make changes if necessary.

  1. Click Book Appointment.
