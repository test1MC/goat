Scheduling an Urgent Consult

The Urgent Consult Request allows for the patient scheduler to assign a room and request an on-call visit from a provider, all from the same screen.

  1. Verify the Urgent Consult Request is selected

  • If the bullet is not green, click Urgent Consult Request.
  • See "Scheduling an Appointment - Patient Scheduler" on page 1 to schedule a non urgent patient appointment.
  1. Click Last Nameor First Name and enter the Patient’s name.
  • The more information entered in the patient search, the more specific the results will be.
  1. Locate the appropriate Patient from the results.
  2. Click on the Patient’s name to select.
  • If unable to locate the Patient in the results, create a new patient:
  1. Click New Patient,
  2. Enter Patient’s information,
  3. Click Create Account when done.
  1. Select the fields for the Urgent Consult
    • Location
    • Service Line
    • Room
    • Enter any Comments if applicable
  2. Select the provider for the consult by clicking Request On-Call Visit.

Note: A notification will be sent to the provider informing them that the patient is ready to be seen.
