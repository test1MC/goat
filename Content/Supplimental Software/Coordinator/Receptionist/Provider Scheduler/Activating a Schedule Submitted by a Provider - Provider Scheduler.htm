Provider Scheduler – Activating a Schedule Submitted by a Provider

If the Auto-assign times check box was not selected when creating a new Provider's Schedule, it will be up to the Provider to select their available times and then submit those times to the Receptionist to make them active. To activate a Provider's submitted schedule:

  1. Locate the Provider's schedule.
    • The Status will be set to Created.
  2. Click anywhere on the line to select it.

  1. Select the times from the submitted schedule by clicking on a time span.
  2. Click the time-frame selected to add it to the Provider's schedule.

  1. Advance through dates to view and add additional times.

  2. Click Activate Schedule when done.

  1. Click Yes when prompted, Are you sure? Activate this schedule?
    • The schedule will now display a Status of Active.
