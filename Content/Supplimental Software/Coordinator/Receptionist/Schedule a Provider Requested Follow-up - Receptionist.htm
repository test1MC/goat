Receptionist – Schedule a Provider Requested Follow-up Appointment

At the end of an appointment, the Provider may request that a follow-up appointment be made with the Patient. This procedure demonstrates how to schedule a Provider Requested Follow-up appointment.

  1. Select the Follow-up Requests tab.

  1. Select the Patient for whom to schedule the follow-up appointment.
  2. Follow the steps in "Scheduling an Appointment" on page 1 schedule the follow-up appointment.
