Privacy Handset

The Privacy Handset is for use in conversations that require privacy or understandability in a loud environment.

When the Privacy Handset is enabled, the standard audio system is disabled. This means the microphone is not active, and the speaker is not active.

When the Privacy Handset is disabled, the audio system is transferred from the phone, back to the standard audio system.

It is important to return the Privacy Handset to the hook on the . Ask someone on the -side to return the Privacy Handset to the hook if necessary.

NOTE: Do not allow the Privacy Handset to hang off the . It must be placed on the hook after use.
