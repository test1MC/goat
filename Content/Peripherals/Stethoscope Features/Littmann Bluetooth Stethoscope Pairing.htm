Installing and Pairing a Littmann Bluetooth Stethoscope

IMPORTANT: It is recommended that only stethoscopes supplied by InTouch Health be used. s purchased through are prepared with power management settings that minimize timeouts.

Note: These procedures are for use with the following InTouch devices: Lite v3, Xpress v2, and Vici.

Option 1 for novice users

  1. Call InTouch Health Technical Assistance Center (TAC), (877) 484-9119 with the PIN and the device's serial number to pair the InTouch device.

Option 2 for advanced users

Prepare the InTouch Health Patient Access Device for Pairing

  1. Select Hardware and Sound.
  2. Select Add a Bluetooth device or, Add a device, from under Device and Printers.
    • A list of Bluetooth devices will be displayed in the window.
Note: Remove any existing from the Bluetooth devices before proceeding.
  • These have a similar number as the one being paired (M3200...), with the last four digits being the same as the PIN.

Complete the Pairing Process

The M3200 stethoscope will appear in the list of Bluetooth devices in the Add a Device dialog box on the InTouch Health Patient Access Device.

Note: Depending on the operating system used by the patient access device, the term used for PIN is either passcode or pairing code. For consistency, PIN is used in this guide.
  1. Select the M3200 device.
InTouch Lite or InTouch Xpress InTouch Vici
  1. Click Next.
    • If prompted, select Enter the device's pairing code, and click Next .

  2. Enter the four-digit PIN displayed on the stethoscope.

InTouch Lite or InTouch Xpress InTouch Vici
  1. Click Next.

    • The stethoscope is paired with the InTouch Health Patient Access Device when the following is displayed:

InTouch Lite or InTouch Xpress InTouch Vici
  1. Call InTouch Health Technical Assistance Center (TAC), (877) 484-9119 to enable the stethoscope on the patient access device.

Confirm Stethoscope Pairing

  1. Turn the stethoscope off by pressing and holding the power button.
  2. Turn on the stethoscope.

    • The stethoscope will be added to the device list.
  1. Select Devices and Printers from the Start menu.
  2. Verify the M3200 device is listed in the Devices.
    • The last four digits of the M3200 device are the same numbers used for the PIN.

  1. Restart the InTouch Health Patient Access Device's software by double clicking the icon on the desktop.
