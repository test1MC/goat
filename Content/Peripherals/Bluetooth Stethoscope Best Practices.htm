Bluetooth Stethoscope Best Practices

Provider Side

  • Provider tests headset connection using the AV wizard prior to starting a session. (Performed out of session)
  • Provider can check the received bandwidth is >1500 kbps in Network Panel of Windows PAS while in session. (Performed in session)
  • Provider should consider using noise canceling headset for best audio experience.

Patient Side

  • For clear auscultation, the patient-side operator must keep the distance between stethoscope and Patient Access Device less than 10 meters with no obstruction and electronic interference.
  • Operator should take care not to stand between the stethoscope and the device.
  • Operator should place the uncovered chest piece directly on the patient skin to reduce ambient noise.
  • Operator should check the battery level on stethoscope and change the AA alkaline battery when there is a quarter of battery remaining.
  • Clean and disinfect the stethoscope earbuds and chest piece before and after each patient encounter.
