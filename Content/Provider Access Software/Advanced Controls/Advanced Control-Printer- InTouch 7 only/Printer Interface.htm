Printer Interface

The Printer tab allows a physician with the Provider Access Software to print an Order (as medicinal, discharge, etc.), Recommendation, Note, or any other informational printout on a printer built-in to most InTouch 7s.

InTouch 7 Printer Operation

  1. In order to print using the Provider Access Software, you must be logged in to an InTouch 7 or have installed the appropriate printer driver on the device. Please see your region’s InTouch Product Specialist for details.
  2. Click on the Printer tab in the Advanced Controls area.

NOTE: The Printer tab is only present after a successful connection to an InTouch 7 configured with a printer. If there is no Printer tab located in the Advanced Controls tabbed area, and you are connected to an InTouch 7 with a printer, please contact InTouch Health technical support.

  1. Once in the Printer tab, type in your Patient’s name, ID, and room number. Click Next.

NOTE: The last 20 patients’ names, IDs, and room numbers are remembered after entry. You may choose to use the drop down list to select recently entered patient information.

  1. Select the printer from the drop down menu of configured printers, select the title for your printout (Order, Recommendation, Notes, or Other).
    • Enter the text and click Print.
    • Click Back to change the patient information entered in , above.

  2. Ensure that the on-site nursing staff or physician removes the printout from the printer and places it in the patient chart per defined protocol. Click Confirm.
