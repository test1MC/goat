To Enable the Boom Camera - Vantage

  1. Click the Aux Video tab of the Advanced Controls section of the Provider Access Software.
  2. Select Boom View to enable the boom camera. When the Boom View is selected, the Home and Reset buttons become active.
    • Click Home to reposition the Boom Camera to a preset PTX position.
    • Click Reset to reset the controllers for the Boom Camera location. If the Boom Camera is moved physically during a session, it can lose track of where it is pointing and a Reset is required. The InTouch Vantage performs a reset whenever a new Remote Presence session is started.

To Enable Dual View

  1. Click Dual View to view two different camera views at once. Current View Advanced Controls-Aux Video
  2. Click Single View to return to a single camera view.

NOTE: Video recording is not supported in dual view.
