Auxiliary Video Inputs

Auxiliary Video Input Ports are available on most Patient Access Devices. InTouch Vantage has a second boom-mounted camera as well. InTouch Lite V3 has a built-in 700 transmission DVI. For HD quality output, refer to To Enable Built-in DVI - Lite V3 on page 1. Select the video input from these devices on the Advanced Controls Aux Video tab.

To Enable the Auxiliary Video Port

  1. Connect an appropriate video device to one of the auxiliary video ports on the Patient Access Device.
  2. Click on the Aux Video tab of the Advanced Controls section of the Provider Access Software.
  3. Click to switch to another Video Camera View.

If the video feed is not available for any reason, you will see the message shown above. Contact someone at the remote location to check that the video device is present and powered on.
