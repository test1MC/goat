TR-1 Stethoscope Device

Depending on the Patient Access Device you beam in to, you may see one of three diﬀerent stethoscope conﬁgurations. The TR-1 stethoscope, installed on the InTouch 7, InTouch 7i, some InTouch Lites, and available on the InTouch Xpress as add-on equipment, is described in this section. Some Patient Access Devices have a PCP Stethoscope or Littmann Bluetooth Stethoscope installed. Refer to “PCP Stethoscope on page 1” or “Littmann Bluetooth Stethoscope Device on page 1”.The Stethoscope tab will let you know which Provider Access Software side stethoscope you need.

 

The TR-1 Stethoscope is an enhancement feature of the Patient Access Device that allows remote auscultation. When you beam in to a Patient Access Device that has a built-in Stethoscope, you will see the Stethoscope tab in the Advanced Controls section of the Dashboard. If you see the Stethoscope tab shown below, you have the TR-1 device and you will need to have the Provider Access Software Stethoscope unit and software drivers installed on your computer.
