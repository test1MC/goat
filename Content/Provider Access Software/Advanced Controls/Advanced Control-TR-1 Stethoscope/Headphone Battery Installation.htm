Headphone Battery Installation

Depending on usage, a single AAA battery lasts about 35 hours. The battery light will start flashing when approximately 5 hours of battery life is remaining.

NOTE: When a replacement battery is needed, use an AAA alkaline battery or a rechargeable NiMH battery. Remaining battery life may vary when using rechargeable batteries.

  1. Tilt the top of the right ear cup inward to gain access to the battery compartment.
  2. Open the battery compartment and insert the positive (+) terminal of the battery first, as shown.
  3. Close the compartment.

NOTES:

  • Not all high quality headphones require a battery.
  • You must use high quality headphones. Contact your InTouch Health Technical Service representative before purchasing or replacing headphones. InTouch Health is a distributor of TR-1 Stethoscopes. Refer to the TR-1 documentation for information on the operation of the stethoscope unit.
