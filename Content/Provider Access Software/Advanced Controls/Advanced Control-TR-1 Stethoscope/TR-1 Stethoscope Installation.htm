TR-1Stethoscope Installation

  1. Plug the USB to Serial Cable into the Null Modem connector and then into the stethoscope unit as shown.
  1. Plug the power adapter into the small round port on the top of the stethoscope unit.
  2. Plug the power adapter plug into an ordinary AC outlet.
  3. Plug the USB end of the USB to Serial Cable into a USB port on your computer. The first time you plug in the cable to a USB port on your computer, follow any prompts to install the required drivers. Drivers should install automatically.
  4. Plug the headphones into the Head Set jack on the stethoscope unit.
  5. Start the Provider Access Software.
  6. Turn the headphones on.
  7. Adjust volume and diaphragm/bell control.
    • Volume to the headphones can be increased by turning the volume control clockwise. Increasing the volume too high can cause distortion in the headphones and will also increase the background noise. Start with the lowest volume necessary to hear the sounds, then turn up the volume as needed to focus in on specific sounds.
    • The B/D Switch can be used in an auscultation exam similarly to a traditional stethoscope. The bell (B) position cuts off the high end at 250 Hz and boosts the low frequency sounds below 100 Hz for enhanced heart auscultation. The diaphragm (D) position slightly reduces the very low frequencies and extends the high frequencies to 1,400 Hz.
