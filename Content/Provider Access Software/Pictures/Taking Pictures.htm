Taking Pictures

Pictures may be taken from the Provider Access Software or the Remote camera.

  • To take a picture, click the Camera icon (Remote or Local).
  • The image will be encrypted and stored temporarily in the Media Browser. Click on the Media Browser to select an image to view.

NOTE: When you end the session you will be prompted to save any media ﬁles that you have not manually saved. If you chose not to save, the ﬁles will be deleted.
