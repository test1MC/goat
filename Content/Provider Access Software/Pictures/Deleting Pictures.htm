Deleting Pictures

  1. View the picture for deletion in the Remote Camera view.
  2. Click on the Delete icon.

All pictures remain in memory until the end of a session and then are discarded unless they are saved.

 

 

 

 

NOTES:

  • This does not delete previously saved pictures.
  • The Patient Access Device cannot be manually driven while a picture is displayed in the Remote Camera view. Return to live video by clicking on the LIVE icon.
