A Disconnect/Dock button Used to Disconnect from a Patient Access Device and to Dock drivable Patient Access Devices.
B Options button Used to open Patient Access Device Options and administrate user accounts.
C Model Indicates model of Patient Access Device currently connected.
D Battery Charge Meter Indicates battery charge level
E Local Controls (Provider Access Software) Local Controls: Image and Video Capture, Brightness, Zoom, Focus, Volume, and Microphone Mute.
F Provider Access Software Camera View (local) Shows local image captured by Provider Access Software Camera and sent to Patient Access Device Monitor.
G Virtual Joystick Vector Key (drivable Patient Access Devices only, not shown) When selected, the key shown at the right pops up, allowing you to slide left or right, backup, and drive forward and/or turn using the mouse. See “Virtual Joystick Mouse-Based Driving on page 1”.
H Advanced Controls Used to access advanced features and applications (Stethoscope, InTouch Vita AutoDRIVE, Aux Video, etc.)
I Media Controls Used to access, save, delete, or share media (images and videos).
J Remote Controls (Patient Access Device) Remote Controls: Image and Video Capture, Brightness, Zoom, Focus, Volume, Audio Mode, and Picture in Picture.
K Remote Camera View Shows remote image captured by Remote Camera and sent to Provider Access Software.
L Mouse Cursor Mode Switches the mouse cursor between Normal, Live Cursor, and Laser Pointer modes. .
M Help button Used to download and open the User Guide.
N Video Mute Turn off video feed to Patient Access side. The Patient Access will see a blue avatar instead of the image of the provider.
