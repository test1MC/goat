Point-to-Drive Video-Based Driving

InTouch Vita can drive to a floor location that you select in the InTouch Vita Remote Camera view with a mouse click or by using your finger on a touch-screen display. A green circle will appear on the display to indicate a valid location is selected and Point-to-Drive is available. The InTouch Vita will attempt to drive there.

  1. When in Normal Cursor Mode, left-click and hold (press and hold on touch screen) for two seconds.
  1. If InTouch Vita is required to adjust its view, you will see this message and you must select the location again.
  1. When a valid destination is selected and set, InTouch Vita will start driving to that location.
  1. Click on the message Driving to destination to stop InTouch Vita. If you stop, you can then select a new location.
  1. When InTouch Vita arrives, the message Arrived at destination is shown on the Remote Camera view on the Provider Access Software.

 

 

 

 

Other messages you may see displayed include the following:

Destination Invalid

You may have selected a location on the Remote Camera view that is not located on the floor or is located outside the area InTouch Vita is allowed to go.

AutoDRIVE Not Available

The most common cause of this fault is that InTouch Vita is not localized. Manually move InTouch Vita to its dock, plug into the dock, wait a few minutes then try again.

Remote Stop Pressed

InTouch Vita has been manually stopped while performing an AutoDRIVE maneuver by someone pressing the Chest Display.

Undocking in Progress

InTouch Vita is docked when a location is selected and a destination set. It will only take a moment for InTouch Vita to undock and continue to the selected location.

Canceled Destination

InTouch Vita has been manually stopped (either from the Provider Access Software or from the InTouch Vita Chest Display) while performing an AutoDRIVE maneuver. If the AutoDRIVE maneuver was selected from the AutoDRIVE menu, the intended destination will be shown on the message.

Failed on Destination

InTouch Vita has tried and failed to reach a set location. This could be the result of a obstruction in the path InTouch Vita needs to take. If the AutoDRIVE maneuver was selected from the AutoDRIVE menu, the intended destination will be shown on the message.
