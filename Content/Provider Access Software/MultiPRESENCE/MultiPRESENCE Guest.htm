The Guests of the MultiPRESENCE session will see a screen like the one below on their Provider Access Software.

The MultiPRESENCE panel on the left side of the screen shows thumbnail images of the other users in the session.The Host will be shown at the top of this list of thumbnail images

The Host will be shown at the top of this list of thumbnail images.

 

NOTE: Only the Host is permitted to take snapshots. Any Guest can still record Remote video.

 

Up to 3 channels of simultaneous audio are allowed: Patient Access Device, Host Provider Access Software, and one Guest Provider Access Software.

If the Push-to-talk mode is enabled for the Guests, only one Guest can speak at a time.

Notes:

  • Microphone volume controls are not available during MultiPRESENCE because the software utilizes Auto Gain Controls, a feature that automatically adjusts your microphone's gain and loudness.
  • Each participant can control the loudness of their speakers by adjusting the speaker slider bar located in the Local Controls' Panel of the Provider Access screen.

In this mode, Guests must either (1) click and hold down the Push to Talk button, or (2) hold down the Shift key while clicking the Push to Talk button, releasing the Shift key will disengage Push to Talk, or (3) Press and hold down the Space Bar or release to disengage.

When a Guest is speaking, the Guest’s speaker bar in the Remote Controls area and the microphone bar and mute in Local Controls area become available for adjustment, if needed.

 

To change the microphone level use the Shift key to lock down the Push to Talk button and then use the mouse to adjust the microphone level slider bar.
