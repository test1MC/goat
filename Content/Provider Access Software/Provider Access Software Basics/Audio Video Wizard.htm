Audio/Video Wizard

The first time you open and log in to the Provider Access Software, the Audio/Video Wizard will automatically launch. The Audio/Video Wizard will lead you through a series of steps to ensure that your camera, microphone, and speakers are properly set up.

NOTE: The Audio/Video Wizard is not available on Windows XP.

  1. Log in to the Provider Access Software. The Audio/Video Wizard will automatically launch. Click Next.

NOTE: If the Audio/Video Wizard does not automatically launch, or you would like to relaunch it open the Options menu located in the top left corner of Provider Access Software screen, select the Audio/Video tab and click on Run Audio/Video Wizard. You can also launch it from the My Care Locations list, with the button on the upper left labeled

  1. Select the video camera that you would like to use from the list of available devices. Verify that you can see yourself. Use the Zoom and Brightness sliders to adjust the camera view and click Next.
  1. Select the microphone that you would like to use from the list of available devices. Speak into the microphone and adjust the Volume slider so you see yellow bars while speaking, as shown below. Click Next.
  2. Select the speaker or headset device that you would like to use from the list of available devices. Click Test and adjust the slider bar to achieve the desired speaker/headset volume. Click Next.

  1. The Audio/Visual Wizard setup is complete. Click Finish.
