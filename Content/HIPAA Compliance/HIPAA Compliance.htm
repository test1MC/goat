HIPAA Compliance

The InTouch Provider Access Software platforms are one of the main components of InTouch Health’s Remote Presence system and are developed with HIPAA compliant safe- guards for use in healthcare. They are available as a pre-loaded Windows Desktop or Laptop, a Soft- ware Install, or as an app for iOS. Connections are supported by the InTouch Telehealth Network; a cloud-based Telehealth server infrastructure that provides secure connectivity and unprecedented flexibility and scalability.
