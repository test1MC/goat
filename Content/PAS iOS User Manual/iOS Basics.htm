iOS Basics

The following is a brief description of your iOS device and some of its controls.

Turning Your iOS Device On

Press and hold down the Sleep/Wake button until the Apple logo appears on your screen.

Turning Your iOS Device Off

Press and hold down the Sleep/Wake button until the red slider appears on your screen. Drag the slider to the right to turn your iOS device off.

Locking Your iOS Device

Press the Sleep/Wake button.

Unlocking Your iOS Device

Press the Home button or the Home button. Enter your passcode if required.

NOTE: It is recommended that your iPad / iPhone be locked with a passcode and the Touch ID to prohibit unauthorized access in the event your device is lost or stolen.

Adjusting Brightness and Volume

Double-press the home button when the iPad / iPhone  is unlocked an swipe to the settings page. Adjust the brightness, lock or unlock the screen orientation, or adjust the volume slider. In addition, the iPad / iPhone volume slider, volume buttons, and the InTouch Provider Access Software Local Settings volume slider may all be used to adjust the volume.
