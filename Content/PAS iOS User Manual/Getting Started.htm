Getting Started

  1. Tap the InTouch Provider Access Software App icon.

NOTE: The Info Menu  on the Tool Bar at the top of the screen is now active.

  1. Enter your Username and Password and tap Login.
    • If your iOS device supports Touch ID, the Provider Access App allows you to login with your recognized Touch ID Fingerprint(s) on your iOS Device. To enable this feature, follow the following steps:
    1. Set up Touch ID on your iOS Device. Instructions on how to do this can be found at: https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201371
    2. Login manually to the Provider Access App with your assigned credentials.
    1. Once logged in, click on the Setting icon on the Tool Bar.

    1. Slide the task bar switch on and it will turn green. Now you can login to the iOS Provider Access Application with your recognized Fingerprint(s). Press the pink fingerprint next to the Login button to login using this feature.
    NOTE: If you reset the password to your Provider Access account, you will need to manually sign-in before re-syncing your Touch ID login.
