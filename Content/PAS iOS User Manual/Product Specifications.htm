Network Infrastructure

A high speed broadband Internet connection is recommended for all Remote Presence connections. The following minimum network properties are required:

Line Quality Requirements
  • Data rate: A connection must have the required up-stream and down-stream bandwidth of 300 kbps up / 300 kbps down.
  • Latency (delay): Average network latency on a connection should not exceed 300ms.
  • Packet loss: A connection must be reliable, without significant packet loss. A connection should experience no more than 3% packet loss.
  • Jitter: Jitter is variability in latency. Jitter on a connection should not exceed +/- 50ms during 95% of the duration of a session.
  • Maximum Transfer Unit: The MTU must not be set below 1400 bytes.
 

WARNING: Cellular connections over 3G networks are not supported!
Data throughput and packet loss requirements can only be achieved over 4G networks.

Wireless Network Requirements
  • The InTouch Health System is compatible with 802.11 a, b, g, and n protocols. Maximum roaming time must be less than 150 milliseconds.
  • In environments which experience network congestion, the InTouch Health application requires Quality of Service (QoS) or priority of traffic to ensure a successful connection.
