Tool Bars

The iPad Tool Bar is located at the top of the iPad screen. The iPhone Tool Bar can be accessed by tapping the . . . icon in the top left corner of the iPhone screen. Tap the X to close the iPhone Tool Bar.

NOTE: The buttons displayed depend on the functionality of the remote device.

Icon Description
Mute Microphone - Tap the Microphone icon to mute the microphone. When muted you will not be heard on the Patient Access Device audio system.
Mute Video - Tap the Mute Video icon to stop displaying your image on the Patient Access Device.
Camera (iPad Only) - Tap the Camera icon to take a snapshot of the Remote Camera view. Use the Media button on the Tab Bar to view, sort, edit, share, and delete snapshots. This option is only available on the iPad.

Aux Video - Tap the Aux Video button to switch the Remote Camera view to a different video camera on the Patient Access Device. If any view other than the Main view is selected, the AutoDRIVE button will be disabled until the Remote Camera view is returned to the Main view.
Privacy Handset - Tap the Privacy Handset button to turn the Privacy Handset on. The standard Patient Access Device audio system will be disabled; this means the Patient Access Device microphone and speakers are not active. The handset is for use in conversations that require privacy or understandability in a noisy environment. When the Privacy Handset is turned off, the audio system is transferred from the handset, back to the Patient Access Device audio system. Any time the stethoscope is on, the Privacy Handset is disabled.
Laser Pointer - Several Patient Access Devices have a laser pointer that allows the remote user to point out items or areas of interest. If you are connected to an Patient Access Device with a laser pointer, the Laser Pointer button will be enabled. When the laser is turned on the Laser Pointer button will turn red .
AutoDRIVE (InTouch Vita Only) - Tap the AutoDRIVE button to select a predefined destination for the InTouch Vita to auto-navigate to. Refer to “InTouch Vita AutoDRIVE on page 1” for further information.
NIHSS - Tap the NIHSS button to use your iPhone to share NIHSS images on the Patient Access Device screen — See “NIHSS Images on page 1”. To share NIHSS images using an iPad — See “Media Manager on page 1”.
Stethoscope - Patient Access Devices with a PCP stethoscope allow for remote auscultation. If you have connected to an Patient Access Device with a built-in PCP stethoscope, the stethoscope button will be lit . When the stethoscope is turned on the stethoscope button will turn blue.
Full-Screen Video (iPad Only) - Tap the Full-Screen button to maximize the video to fit the entire screen. The Tool bar (top of the screen) is still available, but the Tab bar (bottom of the screen) will no longer be available. To return to the Normal Interface, tap the Normal-Screen button . This option is only available on the iPad.
Driving - Tap the Driving button  to use your iPhone to operate a drivable Patient Access Device. To drive using an iPad, use the Driving Tab in the iPad Tab Bar — See “Driving Tab on page 1”.
Info Menu - Tapping the Info button opens the Info Menu. Tap on any menu option to open it. Tap elsewhere on the Multi-Touch display to close the menu option. The Info Menu is located on the Tool Bar.
Settings - Tapping the Settings button opens the Settings Menu. Tap on any menu option to open it. Tap elsewhere on the iPad / iPhone display to close the menu option.
End Session - Tap the End button  to disconnect from a remote presence session. You will be prompted to confirm that you wish to disconnect the session. Tap Yes to disconnect.
