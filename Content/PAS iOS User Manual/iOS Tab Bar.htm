iOS Tab Bar

The Tab Bar shows the additional functions that are available during the current remote presence session. The Tab Bar is only available on the iPad, however several of the Tab Bar options are now available in the iPhone Tool Bar including driving, and sharing NIHSS images.

NOTE: A button will be grayed when the function is not available.

Clinical Apps

The Clinical Apps® Applications require login credentials (username and password) and are available from the InTouch Provider Access Software  or through a Safari web browser. If you are starting StrokeRESPOND or another Clinical Apps® Application from the InTouch Provider Access Software, you can have your credentials automatically presented and verified. Contact InTouch Health Technical Support to arrange for this option. Refer to the online Clinical Apps Users Guide after you login for information and procedures.

Home

Tap the Home button to return to the InTouch Provider Access Software for iOS Dashboard.

Manual Joystick Driving

When connected to an InTouch 7, InTouch 7i, or InTouch Vita, the Driving button will be available to the user. Refer to “Driving Tab on page 1” for further information.
