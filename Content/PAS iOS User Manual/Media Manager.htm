Media Manager

When you tap the Media button the Media Manager is shown in the Tab Display area. If you have not added any snapshots, you will only see the NIHSS Stroke Images which are available on all InTouch Provider Access Software.

 

The InTouch Provider Access Software allows you to take snapshots of the remote video view while in a session. The Media Manager organizes these snapshots in four different ways; All, Albums, Devices, and Dates. This Media Manager is only available on the iPad, but NIHSS images can be access on both the iPad and iPhone.

 

NOTE: Tap the Quick Help button in the Info Menu for the Media Manager help screen overlay.

All: Shows all snapshots individually.

Editing Images

  1. Tap Edit.
  2. Tap an image to select it for editing. A blue check mark  will appear in the bottom right corner of the image. The Export, Delete, and Add To buttons will be enabled, as shown.
    • Export allows you to email an image.
    • Delete removes the image from the InTouch Provider Access Software.
    • Add To allows you to copy the image to an existing album or to a new album.
  3. Tap Done when you are finished editing.

Importing Images from your iPad:

  1. Tap Import, and then tap Camera Roll.
  2. Tap on the desired image to import it.

Albums: Organizes snapshot into albums. Tap an album to view its snapshots. You can not delete images from or import images to the NIHSS Stroke Images album.

Editing Albums

  1. Tap Edit.
  2. Tap New Album to create a new album.
  3. Tap X to remove an album. The images will still remain in the All group.

Editing Images in an Album

  1. Tap on the desired album.
  2. Tap the button, and tap on the desired picture to select it.

The Export, Remove, and Add To buttons will be enabled.

  • Export allows you to email an image.
  • Remove only removes images from the album; images remain in the All group.
  • Add To allows you to copy the image to an existing or a new album.

Devices: Organizes snapshots into albums corresponding to the Patient Access Device which captured them.

  • Tap the desired Patient Access Device album to view the individual snapshots.
  • Tap on Devices to return to all Patient Access Device albums.

Dates: Organizes snapshots into albums corresponding to the date the snapshot was taken.

  • Tap the desired RP Device album to view the individual snapshots.
  • Tap on Dates to return to all Patient Access Device albums.

NOTES:

  • The albums in the Devices group and the Dates group are populated automatically whenever you take a snapshot.
  • Albums cannot be combined.

NIHSS Images

The National Institutes of Health Stroke Scale (NIHSS) images are designed and used for conducting a stroke assessment.

Images in the NIHSS Stroke Images album are provided by default to all users. You can not remove or add images in the NIHSS Stroke Images album or remove the album.

The NIHSS Stroke Images album is located in the Media Manager on your iPad. To access the NIHSS Stroke Images on your iPhone, tap the NIHSS button  in the iPhone Tool Bar.

Sharing Images

  1. Tap an album to open it.
  2. Double-tap on an individual image to share that image with the Patient Access Device.

NOTE: When an image is being shared to the Patient Access Device, it will have a blue border around it in both the Picture In Picture box and the Media Manager display.

  1. To share a new image, simply double-tap another image.
  2. Double-tap on the Picture In Picture box to stop sharing.

Sharing NIHSS Images (iPhone)

  1. Open the iPhone Tool Bar by tapping the Tool Bar button .
  2. Tap the NIHSS images button .
  3. Double-tap on an individual image to share that image with the Patient Access Device.

NOTE: When an image is being shared to the Patient Access Device, it will have a blue border around it in the Picture In Picture box.

  1. To share a new image, simply double-tap another image.
  2. Double-tap on the Picture In Picture box to stop sharing.
