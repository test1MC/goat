InTouch Vita Point-to-Drive Video Based Driving

InTouch Vita Point-to-Drive video-based driving feature allows a remote driver to command the InTouch Vita to a selected spot displayed on the Remote Camera View. When a valid location is selected the InTouch Vita will travel to that spot under AutoDRIVE.

  1. Press a spot for two seconds as shown at the right.
    • If the location is valid, a green target will be shown on the display and the InTouch Vita will attempt to Auto Navigate to the spot.

    If the location is not valid, this icon will shown.

  2. Monitor the InTouch Vita’s progress and follow the message prompts that may appear. Tap STOP on the display message to stop InTouch Vita.

InTouch Vita AutoDRIVE

Each InTouch Vita must have an internal programmed map of its clinical area with predefined destinations in order to use AutoDRIVE. The InTouch Vita can detect its location in relation to the map and move between destinations.

Contact Technical Support to install or modify mapped destinations. Different floors of your hospital may be mapped, but Technical Support must be notified in advance.

NOTE: If the InTouch Vita is in transition between floors, AutoDRIVE will be unavailable before it is docked onto the new floor.

  1. Tap the AutoDRIVE button  on the InTouch Provider Access Software Tool bar to display a list of predefined destinations for your InTouch Vita.
  2. Tap on the desired destination in the list.

InTouch Vita will begin navigating to the selected location and the message Driving to destination will be shown in the Remote Camera view on the InTouch Provider Access Software.


NOTE: Tap the STOP button on the message to stop your InTouch Vita. Once your InTouch Vita has stopped, it can be redirected to the same or to another destination.

  1. When InTouch Vita arrives at the destination, the message Arrived at destination will be shown in the Remote Camera view on the InTouch Provider Access Software.
