iPhone Dashboard

The iPhone Dashboard has three different areas: the Remote Camera view (video from the Patient Access Device camera), the InTouch Provider Access Software Camera View (PIP) (video from the iPhone front-facing camera), and the Tool Bar.



When the Remote Presence session starts, the iPhone Dashboard will show the remote video from the Patient Access Device on the iPhone screen.

The video taken by the iPhone front-facing camera will be displayed in a Picture In Picture (PIP) box at the upper right corner of the screen. The PIP box shows you how you appear to those looking at the Patient Access Device display. Position the iPhone so that your face is centered in the PIP box, and ensure that your face remains centered in the PIP box during your remote presence session.

By pressing and dragging the PIP box you can move it to any corner, or you can tap the PIP box to close it. You can turn this PIP box back on from the Settings Menu. See “Picture in Picture on page 1”.


A separate PIP box is also shown on the Patient Access Device display to show those at the Patient Access Device how they appear on the InTouch Provider Access Software display. You can turn this PIP box off from the Remote Controls section of the Settings Menu. See “Remote Controls”.
