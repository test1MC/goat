Enterprise Login

Enterprise login is enabled for specific hospitals that have been provisioned by InTouch Health. Enterprise login allows Providers to login to the InTouch Health Network using their hospital credentials.

NOTE: Enterprise Login is only available once provisioned by InTouch Health. Speak to an InTouch Representative if you are interested in using Enterprise Login for your hospital.

To login using Enterprise Login, click on the hospital icon on the top right corner of the login screen (shown on the right) and follow these steps:

  1. Enter your domain name. This will be provided to you by InTouch Health.

  1. Click GO. This will redirect you to your Enterprise’s login page.
  2. Sign in using your hospital credentials.

You will now have access to the InTouch Health Telehealth Network.

NOTE: If you forgot your Enterprise username or password, you will need to contact your institution to retrieve them.
  • If you forget your password, tap the red Forgot Password? link.
  • To call InTouch Health Technical support, tap the Technical Support phone number.
  • To open a live chat with InTouch Health Technical Support, tap the Live Agent link. A live chat window will be opened in your default web browser.
  1. After accepting your Username and Password, the InTouch Telehealth Network Connection Wizard will display your login progress. If you experience problems during login, refer to “Login Problems on page 1”.

  1. Once connected to the InTouch Telehealth Network server, the InTouch Provider Access Software will display your list of Patient Access Devices.
    • The color icon next to the Patient Access Device indicates its status:
    • Search the My Patient Access Devices list by tapping in the Search field to open the keyboard. You can also browse through your list of Patient Access Devices by pressing and dragging your finger up and down the list.
    • To logout of the InTouch Provider Access Software, tap the Logout button in the bottom right corner of the My Patient Access Devices window. The InTouch Provider Access Software will return to the login screen.

 

NOTE: The Settings Menu on the Tool Bar at the top of the screen is now active and additional items in the Info Menu are now available.
  1. To connect and start a session, tap on the name of the desired Patient Access Device. The InTouch Telehealth Network Connection Wizard will display your connection progress.
