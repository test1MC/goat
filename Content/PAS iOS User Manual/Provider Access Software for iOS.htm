InTouch Provider Access Software for iOS—Overview

The InTouch Provider Access Software sets a new standard for active patient monitoring and Telehealth workflow management. It is designed to deliver fast, convenient, and effective patient care where and when it is needed. The InTouch Provider Access Software enables you to connect to any InTouch Health Patient Access Device safely and securely, while providing a superior experience when critical patient care matters most.

InTouch Provider Access Software Features

  • Head and camera control of Patient Access Devices using gesture based Box-Zoom and Point-to-See controls.
  • Manual driving control of InTouch 7, InTouch 7i, and InTouch Vita using a virtual joystick. These Patient Access Devices are able to drive untethered over an 802.11 wireless network.
  • Point-to-Drive video-based driving for InTouch Vita allows you to choose and drive to a location on the floor viewed in the Remote Camera View. Choosing a location is as simple as pressing and holding your finger on the desired location on the screen.
  • AutoDRIVE allows InTouch Vita to drive autonomously to a predesignated list of locations using a map of the clinical area.
  • Remote auscultation support on InTouch Vita and other Patient Access Devices equipped with a stethoscope.
  • StrokeRESPOND® Clinical Apps support.
  • InTouch Telehealth Network Wizard supports standards based H.264 Advanced Video Coding (AVC) connections.
  • Automatic bandwidth and video quality optimization.
  • Asynchronous control of brightness and speakers.
  • FDA cleared and HIPAA compliant.

Recommended Accessories

InTouch Health recommends the following products to enhance the usability of the InTouch Provider Access Software. Recommendations are subject to change with each release of a new iPad model.

  • Apple EarPods or Apple AirPods

  • Headset for Remote Auscultation — Remote auscultation requires using high quality headsets such as the Sennheiser PX-200-IIi pictured. For a list of validated headsets, request the publication InTouch Health Communication Device Recommendations.

  • Apple Dock and Portable Charger — Using the Apple iPad dock while using the InTouch Provider Access Software allows you to center your face in the iPad camera. Centering your face in the camera ensures that your face will be centered and looking directly out of the Patient Access Device screen. The 10W USB power adapter and the USB to dock connector cable provide power to the iPad and charge its battery.
